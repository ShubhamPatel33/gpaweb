Fees Payment



Secure payment is our first priority and hence we have adopted Razorpay for the payment module .

Razorpay is a fast and effieient way of making payments and now you don't need to stand in queues and miss any lectures just to pay your hostel fees.

Some IMPORTANT things you should keep in mind:

  • You have to enter the Amount which is told to you by the authority/faculty.
  • You have to give your working E-Mail id as a RECIEPT of your payment will be sent to you which you can further use for authentication.
  • In Semester & Branch field specify your department and Semester number as : Computer6 for Computer branch and 6th semester.
  • Card,UPI,NetBanking,Wallet are accepted.

Click on the below Pay Now button to start your payment.
